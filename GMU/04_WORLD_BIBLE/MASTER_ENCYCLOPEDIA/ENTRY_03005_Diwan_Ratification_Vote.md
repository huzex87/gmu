# The Diwan Ratification Vote and Yusuf's Exposure

## Summary

The Diwan al-Ilm's formal ratification vote, held at the Old Customs House confrontation where Yusuf al-Kanem's true loyalty to "the fourth power" is exposed — the season's climactic convergence point.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Presided by | Elder Kamal and High Cleric Nafisa (ENTRY-01009, ENTRY-01012) |
| Chapters | 17–18 (Episode 9 equivalent) |
| Location | The Old Customs House (confrontation); the Diwan's formal ratification hall (vote) |
| Key admission | Yusuf admits ordering Bashir's murder; denies killing Adamu III, confirms it was natural |

## Description

Three independently developed threads converge here: Fatima's suspicion, Amina's investigation, and High Cleric Nafisa's unease since her Episode 6/Chapter 11-12 encounter with Yusuf (GMU-005 §High Cleric Nafisa). Yusuf is confronted and imprisoned rather than executed or freed — a deliberate loose end for Season Two, per his GMU-005 arc summary. Abubakar is confronted and taken into custody in the same event, though his legal outcome (Chapter 19-20) is distinct from Yusuf's more ambiguous imprisonment, since he held the blade.
