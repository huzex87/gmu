# King Garba's War-Blade

## Summary

The curved mounted-combat blade King Garba Ironhand carries as both a personal weapon and a mark of his standing as a "Blooded" war-captain (GMU-308 §2) — Kasar Dutse's rank earned through survived combat rather than inherited by title.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Type | Curved blade suited to mounted combat, per Kasar Dutse's standard cavalry armament (GMU-308 §2) |
| Owner | King Garba Ironhand (ENTRY-01003) |
| Heraldry | Iron-grey and deep-red hilt wrappings matching Kasar Dutse's crossed-spears-over-stone-peak symbol (GMU-004 §2.2) |

## Description

Kasar Dutse's rank system runs on demonstrated combat record rather than inherited title (GMU-308 §2) — a war-captain's weapon is understood in-world as evidence of that record, not merely equipment. Garba's blade is unornamented by Zamar's ceremonial standards, consistent with Kasar Dutse's "practical, unornamented" military aesthetic (GMU-308 §2); any future concept art or prop design should resist the temptation to make it more decorative than the Military Bible's established doctrine allows.
