# Zamar Cavalry Grounds

## Summary

Zamar's military training and staging ground on the city's outskirts — the visual and thematic opposite of the Royal Palace, and the setting for Malik's early Season One command scenes.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Location | Birnin Zamar outskirts |
| Function | Military training, staging, and command tent for Malik's faction |
| Architectural style | Functional, non-ceremonial — packed earth, wooden fencing, practical structures (GMU-306 §1) |
| Concept brief | `19_CONCEPT_ART/GMU-504_Zamar_Cavalry_Grounds.md` |

## Description

Deliberately built as the Covenant Guard's/Zamar military's practical counterpoint to the Palace's polished ceremonialism — dust and practice weapons instead of lattice and gold, small indigo unit banners rather than full court color saturation. The War Tent variant, with its central map table, is where Malik and his generals plan across the season's early chapters, giving the audience a military-command space distinct from the Diwan's deliberative hall.
