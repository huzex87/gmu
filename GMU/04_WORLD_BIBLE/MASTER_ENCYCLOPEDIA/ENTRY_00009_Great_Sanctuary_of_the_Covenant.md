# Great Sanctuary of the Covenant

## Summary

Birnin Zamar's central place of worship and the seat of the Faith of the Covenant's clergy — architecturally and symbolically independent of all three kingdoms' color systems, despite standing in Zamar's capital.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Location | Birnin Zamar (ENTRY-00001) |
| Function | Seat of Covenant clergy; site of High Cleric Nafisa's public legitimacy address and the Diwan ratification vote |
| Architectural style | Neutral stone tones, restrained geometric carving, grounded in real Sudano-Sahelian mosque architecture (GMU-305, GMU-306 §4) |
| Concept brief | `19_CONCEPT_ART/GMU-506_Great_Sanctuary_of_the_Covenant.md` |

## Description

Deliberately distinct from the Palace's indigo/gold court architecture — a sahn (courtyard) and mihrab (prayer-direction niche) ground the complex in real Islamic architectural tradition per GMU-305's research pass. Its Main Prayer Hall hosts the season's largest public crowd scene (High Cleric Nafisa's Episode 6/Chapter 11-12 legitimacy ultimatum) and, per GMU-305's cross-kingdom decision, represents a faith practiced across all three kingdoms even though the building itself sits in Zamar.
