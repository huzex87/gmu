# Old Customs House

## Summary

A derelict former trade building near Birnin Zamar's river docks, abandoned since before Adamu III's reign — the site of Yusuf and Abubakar's exposure in Season One's climactic confrontation.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Location | Birnin Zamar river docks |
| Chapters | 17–18 (Episode 9 equivalent) |
| Architectural style | Decayed former customs/trade architecture — no kingdom colors present at all (GMU-306) |
| Concept brief | `19_CONCEPT_ART/GMU-510_Old_Customs_House.md` |

## Description

Shares structural DNA with the Kwarafi Merchant House's mercantile function (this was once a trade building) but rendered entirely in decay — weathered grey stone, rotted dark timber, no emerald/white or any kingdom's color present. Fittingly for a confrontation involving "the fourth power" (Yusuf al-Kanem, ENTRY-01005), the building itself has fallen outside all three kingdoms' active control. Fractured moonlight and torchlight through broken shutters defines the scene's visual language.
