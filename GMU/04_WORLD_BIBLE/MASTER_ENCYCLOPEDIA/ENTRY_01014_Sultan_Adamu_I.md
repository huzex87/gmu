# Sultan Adamu I

## Summary

Founder of Birnin Zamar and the first Diwan al-Ilm in 211 CU — a historical rather than a present-day dramatic figure, referenced but not depicted in Season One, whose single founding decision (ratification by the Diwan) still governs every Zamar succession over two centuries later.

## Description

Adamu I is deliberately kept a historical, near-legendary figure rather than a character with scenes — consistent with the "just enough, just in time" worldbuilding discipline (GMU-004 §3's own editorial note against inventing founding-myth detail beyond what's needed). His narrative function across the series is entirely retrospective: every reference to him in dialogue or Diwan proceedings exists to establish the weight of precedent Season One's succession crisis threatens to break. No physical description, personality, or biography beyond the founding act itself is currently canon; this entry should be expanded only if a future flashback or Season Two storyline requires it.
