# Yusuf's Signet

## Summary

A small, deliberately plain ring or seal mark distinct from all three known kingdoms' symbols — first glimpsed in Episode 3, becoming a key visual identifier of "the fourth power" once Yusuf's true loyalty is revealed in Episode 9/Chapters 17-18.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Owner | Yusuf al-Kanem (ENTRY-01005) |
| First appearance | Episode 3 (glimpsed, unexplained) |
| Significance revealed | Episode 9 / Chapter 17-18 |
| Design direction | Small, geometric, deliberately ancient/plain — never elaborate (per GMU-005 and GMU-007 §4) |

## Description

Unlike the ornate seals of Zamar, Kasar Dutse, or Kwara (each tied to their kingdom's locked visual system per GMU-002), the signet is intentionally understated — consistent with Yusuf's entire character design philosophy of being "unremarkable by design." Its plainness is itself a clue in hindsight: it doesn't try to look important, which is exactly why no one questions it until Episode 9. Should be designed once, consistently, and reused across GMU-002 (Brand Bible) and any future Season Two material featuring the fourth power, per GMU-007's existing guidance.
