# Harvest Court

## Summary

The Great Market's busiest ceremonial trading period, held in early Toranen, when the Sultan — or, during an interregnum, the Diwan — traditionally receives formal trade delegations from both other powers.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Observed | Early Toranen, start of the Harvest season (GMU-313 §1, §3) |
| Location | The Great Market, Birnin Zamar (ENTRY-00004) |
| Character | Zamar's most formally ceremonial trade occasion; the one annual event that reliably brings representatives of all three powers to the same place |

## Description

Harvest Court is the Crescent's built-in annual occasion for cross-kingdom diplomacy in a way none of the other festivals are — Founding Day (ENTRY-04001) is Zamar-specific, the Pass Opening (ENTRY-04002) is Kasar Dutse-specific, and the Flood Reckoning (ENTRY-04003) is Kwara-specific, but Harvest Court exists precisely because the Great Market's centuries-old neutral-ground status (GMU-004 §5, GMU-310 §2) makes it the one place all three powers already have standing reason to gather. GMU-313 §3 flags this as a natural setting for a Season Two political scene, since it offers a plausible, pre-established occasion for Fatima, Garba, and Amina's successors (or Amina herself) to appear in the same room without inventing a new reason to convene them.
