# The Merchant-Council of Kwara

## Summary

The rotating governing body of the River Confederacy of Kwara, drawn from the leading trading houses of the river delta's city-states, represented in practice by whichever Speaker currently commands the most votes.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Composition | Leading trading houses, membership tied to commercial standing (GMU-309 §3) |
| Current Speaker | Amina Bello Kwarafi (ENTRY-01004) |
| Seat | Kwarafi Merchant House, Port of Kwarafi (ENTRY-00006) |
| Real power | Major commitments (loans, fleet contracts) require Council approval, not just the Speaker's word |

## Description

Structurally discourages any Speaker from committing Kwara to one political side too early (GMU-309 §3) — Amina's Season One arc of financing both Zamar claimants simultaneously is this institution functioning exactly as designed, not a personal scheme running against it. Funds Kwara's mercenary fleet (GMU-308 §3) on a per-contract basis rather than maintaining a standing military budget.
