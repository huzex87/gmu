# Kantin Rafi (Border Village)

## Summary

A small farming village straddling the Zamar–Kasar Dutse border, thrust inside Kasar Dutse's newly held territory after the Tsauri Pass Seizure — the season's clearest window into ordinary people living with the consequences of a war their leaders started.

## Description

The village's shared well sits at (or near) the new occupation line, staged so soldiers from either kingdom can plausibly occupy the same frame around it — a deliberate visual statement that Kantin Rafi's loyalty belongs to itself and its people, not to either crown. Danladi (ENTRY-01006) takes on a village liaison role here, authorized by Commander Rafiu (ENTRY-01011), giving the audience's ground-level POV character direct contact with the war's real human cost.
