# The Ratification of Sultan Adamu III

## Summary

Sultan Adamu III's succession to his father's throne in 402 CU, ratified by the Diwan al-Ilm without incident — the last peaceful succession the Crescent had seen before his own sudden death 25 years later opens Season One.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Date (CU) | 402 CU (GMU-004 §3) |
| Location | Royal Palace of Zamar (ENTRY-00002) |
| Immediate consequence | Confirms Adamu III as legitimate Sultan; the reign that Season One's crisis follows |

## Description

Recorded in the Diwan's Ledger of Ratification (ENTRY-02007) as an unremarkable, procedurally clean succession — precisely the kind of event the Ledger exists to document and precisely what makes Adamu III's own death in 427 CU (ENTRY-03001) so destabilizing by contrast. Zamar's institutions had gone a full generation without needing to test the forty-day clause; this entry exists mainly to establish that baseline of normalcy against which Season One's crisis reads as unprecedented rather than routine.
