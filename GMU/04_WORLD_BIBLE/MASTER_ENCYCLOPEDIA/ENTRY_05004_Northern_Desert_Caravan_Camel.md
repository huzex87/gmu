# Northern Desert Caravan Camel

## Summary

The desert-adapted pack animal working the northern, pre-pass leg of the Northern Route (GMU-311 §5) — foreign to all three Crescent powers, never bred or fielded by Zamar, Kasar Dutse, or Kwara, and never crossing the pass itself (GMU-317 §6).

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Origin | North of the Sahel Crescent's currently-defined map edge (specific desert polities not yet plot-relevant, per GMU-311's "just enough" scoping) |
| Bred for | Long-distance desert endurance, load-bearing over open arid terrain |
| Primary use | Independent caravan traders working the desert leg of the Northern Route, north of the pass |
| Trade relevance | Carries goods to the pass's northern approach, where they transfer to Kasar Dutse mountain pack animals (ENTRY-05001) for the crossing itself — camels are not suited to the pass's terrain and do not cross it |

## Description

Not a Crescent-native breed and not owned or bred by any of the three powers — this is deliberate: the camel's presence in the world exists entirely to explain how goods travel the desert stretch of the Northern Route before reaching Kasar Dutse's toll point, without implying any of the three powers themselves maintain desert-transport capability they have no narrative or geographic reason to have. Caravan traders using this stock are independent foreign operators, not affiliated with Zamar, Kasar Dutse, or Kwara — their goods become part of the Crescent's economy only once sold or transferred at the pass, at which point Kasar Dutse's toll (GMU-310 §2) and mountain-bred pack stock (ENTRY-05001) take over. Consequently, camels themselves never appear inside any of the three powers' own territory in Season One material — any shot or concept art depicting one should be confined to the pass's northern threshold or beyond it.
