# The Tsauri Pass Seizure

## Summary

King Garba Ironhand's outright territorial seizure of disputed ground at the mountain pass — the season's clearest escalation from opportunism to open (if ambiguously justified) military action.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Actor | King Garba Ironhand (ENTRY-01003), Kasar Dutse forces |
| Chapters | 13–14 (Episode 7 equivalent) |
| Outcome | Garba ends Season One holding disputed ground plus leverage (evidence about Adamu III's death) |
| Casualties | Reported via Commander Rafiu (ENTRY-01011) in the following chapters |

## Description

Marks Garba's shift from watchful opportunist (following Adamu III's death, ENTRY-03001) to active aggressor, per his GMU-005 arc summary. The seizure positions Kasar Dutse as a driver of Season Two tension, since Garba closes the season holding both physical territory and a dangerous piece of information leverage rather than a clean resolution.
