# Location Concept Brief — Old Customs House

A derelict stone building near Birnin Zamar's river docks, abandoned since before Sultan Adamu III's reign — the site of Yusuf and Abubakar's Episode 9 meeting, and Fatima's confrontation with them. The season's climactic confrontation location deserves a distinct visual identity: decayed, secretive, the physical opposite of every polished palace and merchant hall the series has shown.

## Architectural Language

Once-functional customs/trade architecture, now decayed — broken shutters, water-stained stone, overgrown with disuse. Should share some structural DNA with the Kwarafi Merchant House's mercantile function (this was once a trade building) but rendered in decay rather than Kwara's polished emerald/white — dark, neutral, shadow-heavy tones (weathered grey stone, rotted dark timber) with no kingdom colors present at all, reinforcing that this is a space that has fallen outside all three powers' active control, fitting for a scene about the "unseen fourth power."

## Key Location — Meeting Room Interior

**Key elements:** A single large room, mostly empty, with broken shutters allowing gaps of moonlight and torchlight to cut across the space — the shot composition described in the Episode 9 screenplay ("through a gap in the shutters, they see Abubakar meeting with a hooded figure") depends on this fractured-light quality. Dust, cobwebs, disused customs furniture (an old scale, broken crates) as set dressing.

> **AI image prompt:** Derelict abandoned customs house interior, broken wooden shutters casting fractured shafts of moonlight and torchlight across a dusty empty room, water-stained grey stone walls, old broken crates and a rusted trade scale as set dressing, cobwebs, dark and secretive atmosphere, photorealistic interior, high contrast dramatic lighting, tense nighttime confrontation setting.

## Exterior — River Docks

**Key elements:** The building's exterior context — a quiet, disused stretch of the river docks at night, distinct from the working Kwara trade quarter (GMU-507) seen in Episode 4. Should feel abandoned and liminal.

> **AI image prompt:** Quiet disused river dock at night, a derelict stone customs building with broken shutters, still dark water reflecting faint moonlight, abandoned crates and rope along the dock, distant lights of the city visible across the water, atmospheric and secretive, photorealistic wide night exterior shot.
