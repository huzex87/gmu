# Location Concept Brief — Birnin Zamar (City)

Covers the two exterior city settings used in Episode 1: the River Port (Teaser) and the Great Market (Act Two). See GMU-502 for the Palace of Zamar specifically and GMU-503 for the University — both technically part of the city but broken out separately given their scene load.

## Architectural Language

Earth-toned architecture with geometric ornamentation, per GMU-001's visual identity addendum — sun-baked clay and pale stone construction, flat and domed rooflines, lattice windows, covered walkways for shade. Birnin Zamar reads as a river-trade capital: docks and warehouses along the water giving way to denser residential and market districts, with the Palace and University visible on higher ground overlooking the city (both should be composed into any wide establishing shot of the skyline). Population per GMU-004/Encyclopedia: 970,000 across 18 districts — the city should feel genuinely large in establishing shots, not village-scale.

## Palette

Indigo, gold, and white dominate civic and palace-adjacent architecture (Zamar's colors); ordinary residential and market districts should be warmer and more varied — sandstone, ochre, terracotta — with indigo and gold appearing as accents (banners, market awnings, door paint) rather than uniform civic branding.

## River Port (Teaser)

**Time of day:** Pre-dawn, misty.
**Key elements:** Fishing boats with lantern-lit bows already working the water, the city skyline in silhouette behind them (domes, University's terraced white walls, Great Market's gold-tipped minarets), one lit window high in the Palace visible in the distance as the only "wrong" detail in an otherwise ordinary pre-dawn scene.
**Mood:** Calm, ordinary, quietly ominous — nothing in the shot should look dramatic except the single lit window, which the audience should be able to find but not be forced to notice.

> **AI image prompt:** Misty pre-dawn river port in a Sahel-inspired capital city, fishing boats with lantern-lit bows on calm water, city skyline in silhouette behind — domes, terraced white university walls, gold-tipped market minarets — a distant palace with one lit window high up. Warm lantern light against cool blue pre-dawn tones, atmospheric mist, cinematic wide establishing shot, photorealistic.

## Great Market (Act Two)

**Time of day:** Day, overcast/muted (mourning period).
**Key elements:** Normally the city's largest trade hub (per GMU-004 §5, technically neutral ground by centuries-old custom) — for this scene, stalls are shuttered and awnings tied down, unheard of on a normal trading day. Crowds line a processional road instead, dressed in unbleached mourning cloth. Distant ceremonial drums implied by staging even though this is a still/concept reference.
**Mood:** Absence and stillness where commerce should be — the visual wrongness of a shuttered market is the entire point of the shot.

> **AI image prompt:** Large Sahel-inspired capital city market square, stalls shuttered and awnings tied down, unusually empty of commerce, crowds in unbleached mourning-white cloth lining a processional road, muted overcast daylight, somber and still atmosphere, earth-toned sandstone architecture with geometric ornamentation, cinematic wide shot, photorealistic.
