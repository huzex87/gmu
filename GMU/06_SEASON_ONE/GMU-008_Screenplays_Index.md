# GMU-008 — Screenplays (Season One)

**Season One was restructured on 2026-07-11 from 10 full-length episodes (~20–38 min each) into 20 shorter chapters (~15–20 min each)**, to better fit an AI-assisted production pipeline — shorter chapters mean roughly half the shots to generate and review per release unit, while every scene and line of dialogue from the original 10 episodes is preserved unchanged. Each original episode was split into two chapters at its most balanced act boundary. No content was rewritten; this was a structural split only. The 10 original episode files remain in the repository as retired/superseded stubs (see their own files) that point to the chapters that replaced them, so old Document IDs don't silently disappear from the repo's history.
