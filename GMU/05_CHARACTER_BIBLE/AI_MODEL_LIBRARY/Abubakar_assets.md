# Character Design Sheet — Abubakar

Appears in: Background/unidentified, Episodes 01–06; named and identified Episode 07; confronted Episode 09.

## Physical Reference

Man, forties, deliberately unremarkable in the same design register as Yusuf, though slightly warmer/more anxious underneath — a household presence rather than a court one. Medium build, plain features, the kind of face a palace runs on without anyone individually registering him. Unlike Yusuf's polished composure, Abubakar should carry a faint, almost-imperceptible undercurrent of nervousness even in early background appearances, visible only on close rewatch after Episode 9's reveal.

## Costume

Plain Zamar household staff dress — no rank markers, no ornamentation whatsoever. Should be visually indistinguishable from any generic palace steward or servant in early episodes (intentional camouflage), with no costume change even once he's identified in Episode 7, since his ordinariness is the point of his usefulness to Yusuf.

## Expression and Performance Notes

Deferential, competent, forgettable — direct all "menace" budget for this character into Episode 9's confrontation scene only, where genuine fear should break through for the first time ("They're close, Yusuf"). Every prior appearance (background palace shots) should play entirely straight, with no signaling to the audience that anything is wrong — the reveal depends on this restraint.

## Voice Direction

Quiet, deferential, unremarkable in background appearances. Episode 9 confrontation scene: allow real, unguarded fear to enter the voice for the first time — faster speech, audible tension — the single vocal break the character is allowed all season, mirroring the restraint established for Yusuf's design sheet (GMU-405).
