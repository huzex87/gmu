## Production Notes (carried from the original Episode 10 storyboard — covers both Chapter 19 and Chapter 20)

Highest-priority scenes in the entire season: **Scene 6** (the coronation — the single most visually important sequence in Season One, deserving the richest possible treatment of every locked visual asset built across the whole production) and **Scene 10** (the closing balcony image — the final frame of the season, which should be treated with the same care as a theatrical poster image; consider generating this shot in multiple lighting/composition variants before locking one). Cross-reference GMU-508's note that Room 12 (Balcony) should connect visually to the City establishing shots (GMU-501) so the geography reads as continuous across the whole season.
