## Production Notes (carried from the original Episode 8 storyboard — covers both Chapter 15 and Chapter 16)

This episode is intentionally lower-tension by design (the season's "pressure-release" episode per GMU-708's Notes) — favor wider, calmer compositions and warmer, more human-scale framing throughout, in deliberate contrast to Episode 7's urgency. Highest-priority scenes: **Scene 5** (Garba's private council — the season's deepest look at his interiority) and **Scene 9** (Danladi's convergence with the main plot, closing his season-long ground-level arc).
