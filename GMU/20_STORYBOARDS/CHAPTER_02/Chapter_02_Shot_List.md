## Production Notes (carried from the original Episode 1 storyboard — covers both Chapter 01 and Chapter 02)

- **Total shot count:** 91 shots across 19 scenes.
- **Highest-priority scenes for early generation:** Scene 9 (Bashir/Fatima antechamber) and Scene 19 (the discovery) carry the most emotional weight in the episode and are worth generating and reviewing first — if these don't land, no amount of polish on the other 17 scenes will save the episode.
- **Dialogue-heavy scenes** (5, 6, 9, 10, 14, 16, 19) lean on shot/reverse-shot coverage — budget more individual character-consistency checks for these against the AI Model Library sheets, since faces will be generated many times across a single scene.
- **This is a first pass.** Once actual images/video exist, expect to add or split shots — a storyboard drafted from prose rarely survives contact with what a generated image actually looks like, and that's normal.
